Speak the Unspoken

Bridging the gap between silence and understanding with real-time ISL to speech conversion.

Translate Now

Effective communication is a cornerstone of daily life, yet many deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals in India face significant barriers due to the lack of widespread understanding of Indian Sign Language (ISL). This gap limits their ability to engage fully in social, educational, and professional settings. Our web app aims to bridge this divide by converting ISL gestures into clear speech, making communication seamless and inclusive.

In critical settings like education and healthcare, the inability to communicate effectively can lead to missed opportunities and misunderstandings. This app provides real-time ISL to text/speech conversion, empowering deaf individuals to participate fully and confidently in all aspects of life.

This web app represents a significant step toward a more inclusive society, where everyone, regardless of hearing ability, can communicate freely. By leveraging technology, we are helping to ensure that the voices of the deaf community are heard, understood, and valued.
