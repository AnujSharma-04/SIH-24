Usage Guidelines

Welcome to the Usage Guidelines page!
Here, you will find all the necessary instructions to help you navigate and use our application effectively.

1. Getting Started: To start using the application, click on the "Start Camera" button on the home page. This will activate your webcam.

2. Capturing Video: Once the camera is active, you can start recording by clicking the "Start Recording" button. To stop recording, click "Stop Recording".

3. Download Options: After stopping the recording, you will have options to download your recorded video or take a snapshot.

4. Returning to Home: You can return to the home page by clicking the "Close" button. This will deactivate the webcam and take you back to the initial screen.

5. Additional Features: For more features and settings, explore the menu options available on the top navigation bar.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please feel free to contact our support team.
